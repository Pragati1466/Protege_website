Springboard'25

The Mentorship Program of Protege

FAQ's of Springboard

What is SpringCode'25?

A DSA cohort by Protégé IGDTUW offering mentorship, challenges, and a coding community.

Who can join SpringCode'25?

Any student eager to improve DSA skills and prepare for coding interviews.

What kind of problems will we solve?

Problems on arrays, recursion, DP, graphs, and real-world challenges.

How will the mentorship work?

Each mentee gets a dedicated mentor for guidance in DSA and interview prep.

Who can become a mentor?

Seniors with strong DSA skills and internship/placement experience.

Will there be any certificates or incentives?

Top performers will receive exclusive swags.
